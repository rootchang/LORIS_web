LORIS:  A  LOgistic  Regression-based  Immunotherapy-response  Score

The calculator enclosed computes the LORIS score, providing an estimate of the likelihood of response to immune checkpoint blockade (ICB) therapy (including anti-PD-1, anti-PD-L1, an anti-CTLA-4) for different cancer types. This is a research tool (see Original Publication) provided here as a service to the community. Notably and importantly, it is NOT approved by the FDA or any other official regulatory agencies, and by all means, should not serve as substitute for expert professional consultation.
